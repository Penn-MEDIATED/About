Penn MEDIATED

Our Partners

Penn MEDIATED brings together funders, schools, and allied research centers across the University of Pennsylvania to advance the science of the information ecosystem and strengthen the foundations of democracy.

Founding Partners

John S. and James L. Knight Foundation

A founding investment that established Penn MEDIATED as Penn's interdisciplinary hub for media, technology, and democracy research.

University of Pennsylvania

An additional $5 million in combined resources comes from seven schools and centers across Penn, which together form the interdisciplinary core of the Center.

External Partners

Penn MEDIATED collaborates with an array of expert, non-academic partners that are dedicated to addressing the complex challenges impacting the information ecosystem and democracy.

Associated Research Centers at Penn

Penn MEDIATED works alongside a deep bench of related centers and labs across the university studying computational social science, the information ecosystem, and democracy.
